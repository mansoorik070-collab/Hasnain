# Logo Concept Pack

## Goal
Create a bold game logo for **Free Style** that reads instantly on mobile splash screens, thumbnails, and store capsules.

## Concept A — Storm Rift
**Prompt:**
Create a high-impact game logo for the title **FREE STYLE**. The typography is metallic, angular, and battle-worn, with electric cyan lightning cutting through the letters. Add dimensional fracture shards behind the wordmark and subtle ember sparks near the baseline. The mood is rebellious, futuristic, and martial. Background transparent. Emphasize readability for Android app icon usage and splash screen branding.

## Concept B — Dragon Circuit
**Prompt:**
Design a stylized logo for **FREE STYLE** with a hybrid cyber-dragon emblem wrapped around the title. The letters should combine glowing circuitry with engraved martial runes. Use a dark chrome body, cyan edge glow, and orange accent flares. The result should feel like a tournament brand in a collapsing sci-fi world. Transparent background, premium game branding finish.

## Concept C — Arena Neon Crest
**Prompt:**
Create an e-sports style logo for **FREE STYLE** with a sharp crest silhouette behind the wordmark. Use dimensional portals and speed slash motifs. The text should be bold, condensed, and optimized for readability on mobile. Palette: storm blue, white, graphite, and violet. Include a subtle energy crack passing through the center.

## Usage Notes
- Build horizontal and stacked variants.
- Ensure the logo remains readable in monochrome.
- Deliver a clean icon mark derived from the central crest or dragon element.
