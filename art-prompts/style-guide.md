# Free Style Visual Style Guide

## Visual Identity

**Genre blend:** cyber-fantasy martial arts, dimensional collapse, urban mysticism, neon warfronts.

## Core Style Rules

- Time period is **2099**: advanced but battle-scarred.
- Every environment should feel like two realities overlapping.
- Fighters should have distinct silhouettes visible at phone scale.
- Costumes mix tactical streetwear, relic-tech armor, and ritual insignias.
- VFX should use sharp color coding by power family.

## Color Language

- Storm / hero energy: electric cyan, silver-white, storm blue
- Fire / aggression: ember orange, magma gold, dark red
- Void / corruption: black-violet, ultraviolet, deep magenta
- Nature / illusion: petal pink, toxic teal, moonlit green
- Neutral UI framing: graphite, brushed steel, dark indigo

## Composition Rules

- Keep primary character centered or rule-of-thirds aligned.
- Use portal arcs, floating debris, and fractured symbols to reinforce the dimensional-war theme.
- Avoid muddy backgrounds behind UI-heavy screens.
- Maintain strong separation between foreground fighters and background spectacle.

## Material Language

- Nano-fabric wraps
- Cracked luminous plating
- Holographic insignia strips
- Ritual metal ornaments
- Energy seams across gloves, boots, belts, and pauldrons

## UI Art Direction

- Menus should feel premium but fast.
- Favor angular frames, soft animated glows, and shard-like transitions.
- Rank icons must remain readable at 64x64 and 128x128.
