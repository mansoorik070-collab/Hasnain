# Main Menu Background Concepts

## Direction
The main menu background should feel alive, premium, and immediately identify the game's dimensional-war setting without distracting from navigation.

## Concept A — Neon Ash Overlook
**Prompt:**
Paint a futuristic war-torn city at dusk in 2099 with portal cracks in the skyline, drifting embers, rain-slick streets, holographic signs, and distant martial fighters on rooftops. The left side should remain compositionally calmer for menu buttons, while the right side carries deeper visual spectacle. Cyber-fantasy mood, premium game menu art.

## Concept B — Dimensional Shard Hall
**Prompt:**
Create a grand interior hall made of floating shard monoliths, blue-white energy veins, and suspended banners from rival fighting factions. The space should feel ceremonial and dangerous, suitable for a main menu where camera parallax can be added later. Leave negative space for title and button stack.

## Motion Layer Suggestions
- Slow portal pulse
- Floating ash or petals
- Distant lightning flash
- Subtle hologram glitch layers
