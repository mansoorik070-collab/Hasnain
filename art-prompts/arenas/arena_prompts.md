# Arena Prompt Pack

## Neon Ash District
A ruined futuristic city block with holographic signage, fractured roads, distant burning towers, and active portal scars in the sky. Wet reflective ground, strong dueling lane, readable midground.

## Furnace Crown
An industrial forge arena suspended over magma channels with giant chains, molten vents, and fire-reactive steel structures. Heavy orange highlights and black iron framing.

## Zephra's Balcony
A sky palace balcony above cloudstorms with torn banners, glass rails, floating turbines, and dramatic wind currents. Bright but readable for combat.

## Obsidian Bastion
A fortress chamber of basalt metal, beam emitters, heavy columns, and defense cannons. Stark lighting, oppressive military tone.

## Nyx Petal Throne
A corrupted garden palace with glowing petals, mirrored ponds, thorn arches, and illusion mist. Dreamlike but still stage-readable.

## Maw Gate Exterior
A final nexus arena at the edge of a dimensional collapse, with floating terrain slabs, shattered sky geometry, and a colossal gate opening into void light.
