# Boss Intro Art Directions

## Baron Cinder Maw
A massive fire-armored arena boss with furnace vents across his shoulders, molten cracks in heavy armor, brutal brawler pose, blazing forge background, intimidation-first composition.

## Matriarch Zephra
An elegant but terrifying aerial queen standing above a storm balcony with wind ribbons and razor feather constructs swirling around her. Regal silhouette, high-altitude drama.

## Warden Obsidian-9
A towering cybernetic fortress commander with reinforced plating, industrial reactor core, beam cannons, and a disciplined executioner presence. Cold basalt-metal environment.

## Oracle Nyx Bloom
A surreal illusion boss surrounded by mirrored petals, shadow doubles, and lunar garden fragments. Beauty mixed with menace, dream-poison mood.

## Emperor Veyr Null
A final emperor seated or standing before the Maw Gate, wearing shard-infused royal war armor, dimensional energy breaking around his body, mythic final-boss scale, premium key art framing.
