# Loading Screen Concepts

## Direction
Loading screens should teach mechanics, reinforce lore, and showcase dimensional-war spectacle without obscuring progress messaging.

## Concept 1 — Kai Storm Charge
**Prompt:**
Illustrate Kai Storm standing in a shattered neon street in 2099 as lightning coils around one arm and fire gathers in the other. Behind him, broken portals reveal fragments of sky temples, industrial furnaces, and void gardens. Dynamic cinematic lighting, high contrast, premium mobile game splash art, portrait-safe composition with bottom area reserved for loading bar and tips.

## Concept 2 — Tournament of Collapse
**Prompt:**
Show a grand tournament arena suspended above a dimensional abyss with fighters entering from holographic gates. Giant fractured rings float overhead. The visual should communicate scale, danger, and competitive intensity. Reserve screen-safe space for UI tips and loading indicators.

## Concept 3 — Maw Gate Finale
**Prompt:**
Create a dramatic loading illustration of the Maw Gate opening above a collapsing city while Emperor Veyr Null appears as a distant silhouette on a throne of shards. Kai Storm and allies prepare below. Use cyan, violet, and ember lighting. Mobile-friendly readability, strong focal depth, cinematic finish.
